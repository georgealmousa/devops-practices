Follow these six steps to deploy a simple NGINX Ingress Controller;

Deploy ns-and-sa.yaml (Namespace and Service Account)
Deploy rbac.yaml (ClusterRoles and Bindings)
Deploy default-server-secret.yaml (Default Self-Signed SSL)
Deploy nginx-config.yaml (Additional Configs in ConfigMap)
Deploy ingress-class.yaml (Define IngressClass for Kubernetes >= 1.18)
Deploy nginx-ingress.yaml (Define Ingress Controller DaemonSet)
